Planning a visit to Dinosaur National Monument? Vernal, Utah is your gateway city — and Best Western Extended Stay Vernal puts you just minutes away. Whether you're here for the famous Dinosaur Quarry Visitor Center, the canyon scenic drives, or a multi-day outdoor adventure, we're the comfortable home base for your trip.

Why Stay in Vernal?

Vernal is the closest full-service city to Dinosaur National Monument. Staying here means easy access to the monument's Utah entrance, restaurants and grocery stores nearby, and a comfortable hotel room to come back to after a long day outdoors.

Camping inside the monument is an option — but if you want a real bed, hot shower, and reliable Wi-Fi after a day of exploring, Vernal is your spot.

What to Do at Dinosaur National Monument

Dinosaur National Monument spans Utah and Colorado and offers far more than fossils alone. Most visitors wish they had planned at least two full days.

  • Quarry Exhibit Hall — 1,500+ bones in original rock
  • Tour of the Tilted Rocks scenic drive
  • Hiking trails from easy walks to canyon routes
  • Green and Yampa River rafting
  • World-class dark sky stargazing
  • Camping and picnicking throughout the monument

Tips for Your Visit from Vernal

Pro tip: Arrive at the Quarry Exhibit Hall early — it gets busy by mid-morning in summer. Check the National Park Service website at nps.gov/dino for current hours and entry fees before you go.

  • Bring water, sunscreen, and layers for canyon weather
  • Download offline maps — cell service is limited inside
  • America the Beautiful pass covers entry fees
  • Ask the front desk for current road conditions

Other Attractions Near Vernal

Vernal packs a surprising amount into a small area. Don't miss these while you're in town:

Right in downtown Vernal — dinosaur exhibits and fossils, great for families.

Steinaker State Park

Fishing, boating, and camping just north of Vernal.

Adventure UTV Tours

Explore the Vernal backcountry on guided UTV tours — a local favorite.
