Explore the Area Around Naples & Vernal

The Naples–Vernal area is surrounded by some of the most striking outdoor recreation in the American West. Whether you're here for work or play, these destinations are all within easy reach.

🦕 Dinosaur National Monument

~20 minutes from Vernal. World-famous fossil quarry, canyon scenic drives, petroglyphs, and river corridors along the Green and Yampa Rivers.

🎣 Flaming Gorge

~40 minutes. World-class fishing, boating, and the dramatic Red Canyon overlook above the reservoir. One of Utah's great scenic drives.

🏊 Red Fleet State Park

~15 minutes. Swimming, kayaking, dinosaur trackway, and beautiful red-rock reservoir scenery just north of Vernal.

🏛️ Utah Field House

Right in downtown Vernal. Natural history museum with full-scale dinosaur exhibits — great for families and a perfect rainy-day option.

⛰️ Uinta Mountains

~1 hour. The highest range in Utah, with high-alpine lakes, fishing, and wilderness hiking accessible from the Ashley National Forest trailheads.

🪨 Steinaker State Park

~10 minutes north of Vernal. Water skiing, fishing, and camping on a quiet reservoir with easy access from US-191.
