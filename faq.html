Everything you need to know before your stay at Best Western Vernal Inn.

💲 Rates & Booking

Weekly extended stay rates (7 nights + tax): Standard Queen — $588, Double Queen — $660, Standard King — $700. Monthly rates and long-term corporate billing are also available — call (435) 789-6625 for pricing.
Yes! Monthly rates are available for long-term guests, oil field workers, contractors, and corporate travelers in the Uintah Basin. Company billing arrangements can also be discussed. Call (435) 789-6625 for monthly pricing and availability.

🍳 Dining

Yes! A free hot breakfast is included with every stay, served daily. Start your morning with warm dishes, fresh pastries, pancakes, fruit, coffee, and more — all on us, every single day.

🐾 Pet Policy

Yes, we welcome up to two dogs per room with an 80 lb limit per dog. Other pet types (such as cats) may be allowed with prior hotel approval — please contact us before arrival.

Pet fees: $30.00 USD per day, plus a refundable $100.00 damage deposit due at check-in. If damage occurs or excessive cleaning is needed, the deposit may become non-refundable and additional charges may apply.

🕒 Check-In & Check-Out

Check-in time is 3:00 PM. If you arrive early, we'll do our best to accommodate you — just give us a call at (435) 789-6625.
Check-out time is 11:00 AM. Late check-out may be available upon request — contact the front desk the evening before your departure.

📶 Connectivity & Parking

Yes — fast, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the entire hotel and in every guest room. No passwords to hunt down, no fees, no limits.
Yes — complimentary parking is available for all guests including trucks and work vehicles. Pull right up to your room, no fees, ever.

🛏️ Rooms & Suites

Most of our rooms include a full kitchenette with a stovetop, microwave, full-size refrigerator, and coffee maker — perfect for extended stays.
Yes! We offer both King Jacuzzi Suites and Jacuzzi Double Queen Suites — featuring a private in-room soaking tub. Call us to check availability: (435) 789-6625.
Yes — we offer ADA accessible king rooms with wide doorways, roll-in showers, lowered fixtures, and ground-floor access.

📍 Location

Best Western Vernal Inn is located just 0.6 miles from Vernal Regional Airport (VEL) at 1935 South 1500 East, Vernal, UT 84078.
Dinosaur National Monument is approximately 20 minutes from our front door. We're an ideal base camp for exploring Dinosaur Country.

Still Have Questions?

Our front desk team is here 24 hours a day — give us a call or send us an email and we'll be happy to help.
