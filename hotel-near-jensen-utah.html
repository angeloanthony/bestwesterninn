Jensen is a small community on US-40, best known as the gateway to Dinosaur National Monument — home to one of the largest dinosaur fossil quarries in the world. There are no hotels in Jensen itself, which makes Best Western Vernal Inn your closest and most comfortable option.

We're just 15 minutes west on US-40 — close enough to make an early morning run to the Quarry Visitor Center before the crowds arrive, and comfortable enough to come back to after a full day on the trails.

✦ Gateway to Dinosaur Country

Jensen, Utah —
Where the Fossils Are.

Jensen, Utah sits right at the entrance to Dinosaur National Monument, one of America's most remarkable paleontological sites. The Quarry Exhibit Hall protects over 1,500 dinosaur bones still embedded in the cliff face — visible exactly as they were discovered.

From Best Western Vernal Inn, you're perfectly positioned to explore everything Jensen and the surrounding area has to offer:

  • Dinosaur National Monument — Quarry Visitor Center
  • Quarry Exhibit Hall — 1,500+ fossils in the rock face
  • Split Mountain Campground & Green River
  • Jones Hole National Fish Hatchery
  • Cub Creek Road scenic drive
  • Petroglyphs and ancient rock art sites
  • Green River rafting and kayaking put-ins
