The Uintah Basin Energy Landscape

The Uintah Basin sits atop one of the most significant hydrocarbon-bearing formations in the Rocky Mountain region. Oil and gas production, pipeline infrastructure, and associated services have driven the local economy for decades. Vernal serves as the operational hub — where crews base out of, where supplies are sourced, and where the workforce lives between rotations.

If your project is anywhere in the basin — from the Wasatch Front to the Colorado border, from the Wyoming line down to the Book Cliffs — Vernal is almost certainly your closest full-service city, and Best Western is your most complete lodging option in it.
